# Securing your Android phone
_In today's modern world, a smartphone is a key to access a whole range of different aspects of the internet and the world around us, but should we trust in the safety of our data that is stored on a smartphone?_

There are many ways in which devices such as smartphones and tablets can be unsafe or leak information about who or where we are, or reveal when or how we are using it. However there are also many ways in which we can secure ourselves from these things.
## Problems and Solutions
Here are some of the problems associated with smartphones. Since there are quite a few, I will not spend too long looking at each one.
### Malware
Android phones run a version of Linux, and although Linux is generally considered a secure operating systems there are always problems with any system. Problems most often occur in older models of phones, as they are often unsupported by the company, meaning there will be no more updates to their security systems.
