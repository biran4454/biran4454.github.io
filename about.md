# About my Blog
This blog is intended to be a useful resource for the average person to improve their privacy and security in the online world. I do not expect many people to find my blog, but to those who do, I hope that this will help you to understand and take action to secure your data and privacy. This blog will mainly be so that I can refer friends and family to it's content in the case that anyone is interested in the quest for safety online.
Although there is a lot of content (or there will be soon), I have tried to organise it into small, edible chunks such that they can be read in under five minutes, and not be too overwhelming. In addition I have also tried to use everyday vocabulary where I can, such that it isn't too confusing to those who focus less on the complexities of computers.
This blog is by no means complete, and I still have much to learn. I therefore advise that if one is in a situation where safety, security, and privacy is essential, one should not use my blog as an extensive source of knowledge, and instead look elsewhere on the internet.
I welcome any comments you may have, especially corrections to my blog in the case that there is inaccurate information or spelling errors. You can contact me at biran4454@protonmail.com.
